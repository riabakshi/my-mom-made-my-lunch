About Us

My name is Ria, and I created this website. All the recipes featured on this website have been in my family, passed through my Nani (Maternal Grandmother), to my mom, to me, collecting their own little twists with each generation. My mom (pictured on the left) is the current chef for all the listed recipes.

These are dishes that have been a steady part of the my diet at home. My comfort food is toor daal and rice. On holidays, I always look forward to eating my mother's Besan ladoo deserts, melting in my mouth.

When my brother moved out of the house, it was harder for him to take this cooking with him. He took some Indian snacks with him, refrigeratable food, and the Indian equivilant of ramen called Maggi.
I hope these foods will contribute to a long lasting memory, and that you make your own little changes to each recipe. Click through the navigation bar to find out different recipes and more.
